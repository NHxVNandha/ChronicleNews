The Supremacy of the Grid

The grid is the journalist's greatest ally. By adhering to a strict columns-and-gutters system, we eliminate the ambiguity of hierarchy. Every element has its place, and every place has its element. This isn't about restriction; it's about clarity. When the design stops competing for the reader's attention, the content can finally speak.
